module  moment
 In this module various moments of the density distribution are calculated. Most of them come in two versions: an isospin-dependent one characterized by a final isospin index of dimension~2, and a summed one distinguished by the ending "\c tot" in its name. Thus, e.g., three variants of center of mass are stored: that of the neutrons cm(1:3,1) , of the protons cm(1:3,2), and of the total mass distribution cmtot(1:3) .
