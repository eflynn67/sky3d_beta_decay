module \mbox{\hyperlink{namespacemoment}{moment}}
\begin{DoxyCompactList}\small\item\em In this module various moments of the density distribution are calculated. Most of them come in two versions\+: an isospin-\/dependent one characterized by a final isospin index of dimension$\sim$2, and a summed one distinguished by the ending \char`\"{}\textbackslash{}c tot\char`\"{} in its name. Thus, e.\+g., three variants of center of mass are stored\+: that of the neutrons {\ttfamily  cm(1\+:3,1) }, of the protons {\ttfamily  cm(1\+:3,2)}, and of the total mass distribution {\ttfamily  cmtot(1\+:3) }. \end{DoxyCompactList}\end{DoxyCompactItemize}
